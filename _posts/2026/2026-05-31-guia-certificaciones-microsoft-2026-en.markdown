In this session, together with Pablito Piovano, we share a practical guide to understanding how to approach Microsoft certifications in 2026, both for people who are just getting started and for those who want to better organize their next professional step.

It is a good starting point if you want to review learning paths, identify which certifications make the most sense for your profile, and get a clearer idea of where to begin without getting lost among so many options.
